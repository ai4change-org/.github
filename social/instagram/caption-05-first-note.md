## Caption

Every movement starts with a single note.

Playing for Change started with one man singing on a street corner in Santa Monica. Roger Ridley didn't know that his voice would be layered with musicians from 60+ countries. He just sang because he had to.

AI4Change started the same way — with one developer watching that video late at night and thinking: "What if we could do this with solutions?"

That thought became a note in a journal. Then a sketch. Then a GitHub organization. Then a manifesto. Then a community.

Now it's your turn.

Maybe your note is a problem your community faces every day. Maybe it's a skill you've been wanting to use for something that matters. Maybe it's sharing this post with someone who has a problem worth solving.

Whatever it is — it matters. Because somewhere in the world, someone else has a piece of the solution. A skill you don't have. A perspective you haven't considered. A way of using AI that hasn't occurred to you yet.

Alone, you have a problem.
Together, we have a song.

Every solution starts with a problem. Every song starts with a single note.

What's yours?
