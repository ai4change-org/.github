## Caption for This Carousel

AI is the most powerful tool humanity has ever created. But the people who need it most are the least likely to shape how it's used.

AI4Change is an open-source movement that connects real communities with global contributors who use AI to build solutions together. Every project is a "song." Every contributor is a musician.

We don't start with the technology. We start with the problem. And the community decides what gets built.
