# 💚 Getting Help with AI4Change

Thanks for being part of the AI4Change community! Here's how to get the help you need.

## 🗣️ Community Support

| Channel | Best For |
|:--------|:---------|
| [**GitHub Discussions**](https://github.com/orgs/ai4change-org/discussions) | Questions, ideas, general conversation |
| [**Problem Issues**](https://github.com/ai4change-org/problems/issues) | Submitting real-world problems for the community to solve |
| [**Website**](https://ai4change-org.github.io) | Overview, getting started, trending problems |

## ❓ How to Ask a Good Question

1. **Search first** — Check existing discussions and issues
2. **Be specific** — Include what you tried, what happened, and what you expected
3. **Add context** — Share your environment, repo, or relevant links
4. **Be patient** — We're a volunteer community; responses may take time

## 🐛 Reporting Bugs

- **Website bugs**: Open an issue on [ai4change-org.github.io](https://github.com/ai4change-org/ai4change-org.github.io/issues/new)
- **Problem tracker bugs**: Open an issue on [problems](https://github.com/ai4change-org/problems/issues/new)
- **Security issues**: See our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) — **do not** open public issues for vulnerabilities

## 🤝 Contributing

Want to help build AI4Change? Here's how:

- **Submit a problem** — Share a challenge from your community: [Submit here](https://github.com/ai4change-org/problems/issues/new)
- **Join a discussion** — Help shape which problems we tackle: [Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/ai4change-org/discussions)
- **Contribute code** — Pick up an issue and start building
- **Spread the word** — Star our repos, share with your network
