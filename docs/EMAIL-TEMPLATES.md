## 1. Launch Announcement — Personal Network

**Subject:** I started something. Would love your eyes on it.

---

Hey [Name],

I wanted to share something I've been working on.

You know Playing for Change? The project where they recorded musicians independently across the world — a guitarist in New Orleans, a drummer in Mumbai, a choir in Soweto — and mixed them into one song? None of them had met. The result was something no single studio could produce.

I've been building **AI4Change** — the same idea, applied to solving real-world problems with AI.

The concept: real communities submit real problems (crop disease prediction, literacy tools, disaster response). Global contributors use AI as their instrument to build open-source solutions together. Each project is a "song." Each contributor is a musician.

It's live on GitHub: https://github.com/ai4change-org

We're in the early days — collecting the first wave of problems from communities worldwide. I'd love it if you:

- **Took a look** and told me what you think (honestly — tear it apart if needed)
- **Starred the repo** if it resonates (helps with visibility)
- **Shared it** with anyone who might have a problem to submit or skills to contribute
- **Submitted a problem** if you know of a real challenge a community faces

The manifesto has the full story: https://github.com/ai4change-org/.github/blob/main/docs/MANIFESTO.md

No pressure. Just wanted to share something I care about with people I respect.

Thanks,
[Your name]

## 2. Partnership / Collaboration Outreach

**Subject:** AI4Change — open-source AI solutions, built by the world

---

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because [Organization]'s work in [their focus area] aligns closely with something we're building.

**AI4Change** is an open-source movement that connects real-world problems — submitted by communities — with global contributors who use AI to build solutions together. Inspired by Playing for Change (the project that connected musicians across continents into collaborative songs), we apply the same model to AI-powered problem-solving.

**How it works:**
- Communities submit real problems (agriculture, health, education, environment, disaster response)
- Global contributors — developers, designers, domain experts — use AI as their multiplier
- Solutions ship open source, documented, and free

**Why I'm reaching out to you:**

[Choose one or adapt:]
- Your organization works directly with communities facing [specific problem area]. We'd love to explore how those communities could submit problems to AI4Change and how our contributors could support solutions.
- Your team has deep expertise in [area]. We're building our first pilot projects and would benefit enormously from [their specific expertise].
- You've built [specific thing they've done]. We share the same belief that [shared value]. I think there's a natural collaboration here.

We're in Phase 1 — collecting seed problems and building community. We're not asking for funding or formal commitments. Just a conversation about whether our missions might intersect.

Would you have 20 minutes in the next couple of weeks?

## 3. Thank You for Contributing

**Subject:** You're part of the song now.

---

Hey [Name/Handle],

Thank you for [specific contribution — submitting a problem / joining the discussion / starring the repo / your first PR / spreading the word].

AI4Change is in its earliest days. Every contribution right now — no matter how small it seems — is foundational. You're not just contributing to a project. You're helping prove that strangers from different continents, each using AI as their instrument, can build something extraordinary together.

Playing for Change started with one man singing on a street corner. AI4Change started with one developer watching that video. And now it's growing — because of people like you.

**What happens next:**
- We're collecting seed problems from communities worldwide ([submit one](https://github.com/ai4change-org/problems/issues/new) if you know of a real challenge)
- Community discussions are happening in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/ai4change-org/discussions) — your voice matters there
- Phase 2 (Community) launches in April — that's when we start identifying candidates for the first "song"

If there's anything you need, anything that's unclear, or anything you think we should do differently — say so. This is your movement as much as anyone's.

Every solution starts with a problem. Every song starts with a single note. You just played yours.

Welcome to the band.

[Your name]
