## Caption

Two realities exist right now:

Reality 1: Another AI startup raises $50M to build a smarter to-do list.

Reality 2: A farmer in sub-Saharan Africa loses 40% of her crops to a disease she could detect with a smartphone camera — if someone built the tool.

AI is the most powerful technology humanity has ever created. A teenager with a laptop can now do analysis that used to require a PhD and a six-figure budget.

But the people who need AI the most are the least likely to shape how it's used. They're not in the boardrooms, the research labs, or the demo days.

AI4Change exists to close that gap. We connect real communities with global contributors who build AI-powered solutions together — open source, free forever.

The community decides what gets built. Not a board. Not a VC. Not an algorithm. People who live the problem decide.

We're collecting problems now. Agriculture, education, health, environment, disaster response.
