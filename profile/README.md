# 🌍 AI4Change

### AI as the Great Equalizer

**Real people from around the world, each using AI as their instrument<br>to solve humanity's most pressing problems. Together. In the open.**

---

*Inspired by [Playing for Change](https://playingforchange.com) — they proved music connects people across borders.<br>We believe AI can do the same, but with solutions.*

---

### How It Works

| Step | What Happens |
|:----:|:-------------|
| 🗣️ | **Post a Problem** — Anyone, anywhere submits a real-world challenge |
| 📊 | **Community Signals** — Upvotes, discussion, AI aggregation surface what matters |
| 🔥 | **Trending Board** — The world sees what needs solving most |
| 🔨 | **Build Together** — Contributors join, each using AI as their multiplier |
| 📦 | **Ship Open Source** — Free, documented, a building block for the next project |
| 🎬 | **Tell the Story** — A Song Around the World, but for solutions |

---

### The Parallel

| 🎸 Playing for Change | 🤖 AI4Change |
|:----------------------|:-------------|
| Musicians from around the world | Problem-solvers from around the world |
| Each plays their own instrument | Each uses AI as their instrument |
| Mixed into one seamless song | Integrated into one open-source solution |
| Street musician = Grammy winner | Farmer = Fortune 500 analyst |
