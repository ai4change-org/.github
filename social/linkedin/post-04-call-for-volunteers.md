## Post Copy

We're looking for people who build things that matter. And you don't need to be a developer.

AI4Change is an open-source movement connecting real-world problems with global contributors who use AI as their instrument to build solutions. We need all kinds of "musicians" for this band:

PROBLEM SUBMITTERS
You live in a community facing a real challenge — agriculture, health, education, environment. You don't need a solution. You need to describe the problem. Your lived experience is the most valuable contribution anyone can make.

SOLUTION BUILDERS
Developers, data scientists, ML engineers. You build things. You want those things to matter. You'll use AI as your multiplier alongside contributors from around the world.

DESIGNERS
UX/UI people who care about real-world usability. Our solutions need to work for a farmer on a low-cost phone, not just a developer on a MacBook.

STORYTELLERS
Writers, filmmakers, documentarians. Every AI4Change project is a "Song Around the World" — and someone needs to tell the story of how strangers from different continents built something extraordinary together.

DOMAIN EXPERTS
Agronomists, public health professionals, educators, disaster response specialists. Your expertise keeps AI grounded in reality.

TRANSLATORS
Our community spans the globe. Translating problems and solutions into your local language removes barriers for the next contributor.

We're in our founding phase. The first "song" — our first pilot project — will be chosen by the community. Every early contributor shapes what this becomes.

You don't need permission. You need a problem, a skill, or the desire to build something that matters.

Get started: https://github.com/ai4change-org
Join the conversation: https://github.com/orgs/ai4change-org/discussions
