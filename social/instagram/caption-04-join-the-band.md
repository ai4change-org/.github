We're assembling a band. And every instrument matters.

AI4Change needs all kinds of contributors to build open-source AI solutions for real-world problems:

You speak multiple languages? Translate problems and solutions so the next contributor isn't blocked by a language barrier.

You don't need to be an AI expert. You don't need a degree or permission. You need a problem, a skill, or the desire to build something that matters.

The first "song" is coming. Every early contributor shapes what this becomes.
