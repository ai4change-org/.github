## Caption

At AI4Change, we don't build products. We compose songs.

Here's what that means:

The code is the melody — the core functionality that solves the problem.
The data is the rhythm — local knowledge, community context, real-world feedback.
The documentation is the harmony — making the solution accessible to everyone.
The story is the hook — the human narrative that inspires the next "song."
The contributors are the musicians — each playing their part, each essential.

This isn't just a cute metaphor. It's how we think about everything.

Playing for Change proved that when you record musicians independently across the world and layer their parts together, the result is something no single studio could produce. Ordinary people, connected by shared purpose, creating something extraordinary.

We're doing the same thing. With AI. With open source. With problems that actually matter.

Each project is a composition. The audience is every community that needs a solution but was never invited to the table.

Our first "song" is coming. The community decides what it will be.

What instrument do you play?
