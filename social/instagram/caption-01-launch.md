## Caption

Late one night, three rabbit holes deep in YouTube, we clicked on Playing for Change's "Stand By Me."

Musicians from across the world — a guitarist in New Orleans, a drummer in Mumbai, a choir in Soweto — each recorded independently, blended into a song no single studio could produce.

One thought wouldn't let go: "What if we could do this with solutions?"

Today, AI4Change is live.

An open-source movement where real communities submit real problems, and global contributors use AI as their instrument to build solutions together. Every project is a "song." Every contributor is a musician. Every solution is open, free, and built by the world.

We're collecting our first problems now — from farmers, teachers, health workers, and community leaders worldwide.

The first "song" hasn't been chosen yet. The community decides.

Every solution starts with a problem. What's yours?
